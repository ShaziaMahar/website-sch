STATE OF THE ART CLASSROOM

Jeejal Amaan Public School boasts a state-of-the-art infrastructure

while the auditorium sets the stage for grand performances. The ICT lab is a digital realm of exploration, complemented by vast classrooms and a cutting-edge

CULTURAL HUTS (KACAHRI JO MAAG)

Cultural Tapestry Woven at JAPS School
Nestled within the JAPS School grounds, a vibrant cluster of Sindhi rural
cultural huts has emerged. Stepping inside, one is transported to a bygone
era, greeted by the warmth of earthen floors and the rustic elegance of
hand-woven carpets. These huts are more than mere exhibits; they're a thriving hub for cultural
activities. Children gather to learn the art of Ajrak block printing, their
fingers dancing across vibrant dyes. Melodies of Sindhi folk songs fill the
air as students strum the sitar and sway to the rhythm of the dhol. Elders,
repositories of ancient wisdom, share stories of brave heroes and mythical
creatures, their voices weaving a spellbinding tapestry of tradition.
JAPS School's cultural huts are not just bricks and mortar; they're a testament
to the enduring spirit of Sindh, a living bridge connecting generations, and a vibrant
stage where the rich tapestry of Sindhi culture comes alive.

PICK & PAY ZONE

The school has a tuck shop with title “Pick & Pay Zone” where basic stationery,
cookies and snakes are available for the student. Its specialty is to self shop.
The student pick their required product, self calculate its value and drop the required
amount in the payment box, maintain high value of integrity.
